# Privacy Policy — My Pregnancy

_Last updated: 21 July 2026_

My Pregnancy ("the app") is a pregnancy-tracking companion. This policy explains
what the app does and does not do with your information. Please read it with the
in-app note that the app is **for education and organization only and does not
replace medical advice**.

## The short version

- Your pregnancy data stays **on your device**. The app has **no account, no
  login, and no analytics or tracking**.
- The developer does **not** collect, receive, store, or sell your data.
- The only time data leaves your device is when **you** use an optional AI
  feature that **you** configured with **your own** AI provider key — and then it
  goes directly from your device to **your** provider, not to us.

## What the app stores (on your device only)

Everything you enter or attach is saved locally in the app's private storage:
your pregnancy profile, timeline, symptom journal, medicines, appointments,
weight, blood pressure, kick/contraction logs, hospital-bag checklist, and any
report photos or files you attach to Records. This data is not transmitted to
the developer.

## Optional AI features (bring-your-own key)

The app's AI features — reading a scan/lab report and the "Ask" assistant — are
**off by default**. If you choose to turn them on, you enter your own AI provider
endpoint and API key (for example, your Microsoft Azure OpenAI deployment). Your
key is stored in your device's encrypted secure storage.

When you actively use an AI feature, the relevant input (a report image you
selected, or a summary of your own tracked data for a question you asked) is sent
**directly from your device to the AI endpoint you configured**, to generate the
result. That processing is governed by your AI provider's terms and privacy
policy. The developer of this app does not operate that endpoint and does not
receive, log, or store this content.

If you never enable AI, no data ever leaves your device.

## Permissions

- **Camera / Photos** — only to let you attach or photograph report pages you
  choose. The app does not browse your library on its own.
- **Notifications** — to show the reminders you set (medicines, appointments,
  optional kick reminder).

## Backups

Any backup is **initiated by you** via the Export option, which creates a file
you choose where to send or store (e.g. your own cloud drive or messaging app).
The developer is not involved in and cannot access your backups.

## Children

The app is intended for expecting parents and is not directed at children.

## Data sharing and sale

We do not share or sell your data. There are no third-party advertising or
analytics SDKs in the app.
