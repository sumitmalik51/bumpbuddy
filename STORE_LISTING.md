## Full description (max 4000 chars)
My Pregnancy is your week-by-week companion for a calmer, better-organised
pregnancy — with the best twin support of any pregnancy app, and optional AI that
reads your scan reports for you.

WHAT MAKES IT DIFFERENT
• Built for twins as well as single babies. Tell it your chorionicity
  (DCDA / MCDA / MCMA) and the whole app adapts — scan schedule, typical arrival
  window, and per-baby growth.
• Optional AI scan reading (bring your own AI key): photograph an ultrasound
  report and it fills in each baby's measurements — EFW, HC, AC, FL, placenta,
  fluid — and shows the twin weight difference. It reads blood/lab reports too.
• "Ask": a private assistant that answers questions about YOUR own tracked data,
  in English or Hindi.

EVERYTHING IN ONE PLACE
• Week-by-week timeline with development, tests due and tips
• Beautiful week-by-week baby visuals
• Growth charts with reference percentiles; scan-over-scan comparison
• Kick counter and a labour contraction timer (5-1-1 aware)
• Blood pressure, weight and water tracking
• Medicine and appointment reminders
• Records vault for scans, reports and prescriptions
• Twin-aware hospital-bag checklist
• Common-questions library
• Doctor-visit summary you can share
• Full backup you control

PRIVATE BY DESIGN
Your data stays on your device. No account, no ads, no tracking. AI features are
optional and use your own provider key.

Available in English and हिन्दी.

My Pregnancy is for education and organisation only and does not replace medical
advice. Always follow your doctor's guidance.
